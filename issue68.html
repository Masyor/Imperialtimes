ADDITIONAL MOON OF HOMINIES DISCOVERED

From: beardog2150

While in these pages we often report on the great feats of heroism and martyrdom performed by the brave soldiers of the Imperium, we must also remember the equally vital efforts of the Administratum, without whose tireless record-keeping there would be no tracking of tithes, no monitoring reports from myriad campaigns, and ultimately no timely dispatch of men and materiel to where they are needed. Thus, let us take a moment to reflect on an amazing discovery, that being an additional moon of the Scribe World of Hominies!

Hominies serves as a vital clearing-house for information not just for its home sector, but for several neighboring sectors, making up a substantial part of the entire segmentum's record-keeping. The reams of paper transcribed daily are awe-inspiring, and after construction of innumerable archives to preserve them on Hominies itself, the planet's barren moon was transformed into a veritable monument to bureaucracy, being riddled with labyrinthine tunnels for ever-more records - and that capacity is nearly filled, throwing the entire operation of these several sectors into doubt!

Enter, a local peculiarity, that this moon's course appeared to be highly erratic. It was only thanks to the devoted labors of a regiment of data-savants that it was pieced together that there is, in fact, a second moon, which had never been officially recorded, and was simply assumed to be a stubbornly suspect data-phantom that could never be quite purged from their systems, and any sightings or other measurements simply mistakes. Indeed, the urban legend 'Hominies has two moons' was famed as a story to horrify these devoted record-keepers!

Imperial Prefects are overjoyed at the presence of an entire additional moon to store records on, and already construction efforts have begun. Duly, the new moon has been named 'Hominies Minor Minor', and will ensure the safe archiving of vital information for centuries to come!

BEWARE OF BLUESKIN TRICKERY

From: mourhurst

Citizens of the Imperium, beware of the alluring lies of the T’au. Their so-called “Greater Good” is but a mask, their true intent is the subjugation of all who do not kneel before the alien to become their slaves. Reports from the fringe worlds reveal entire populations vanished, replaced with conscripts for their endless wars. The T’au are not a friendly xenos species, they are liars, manipulators, and deceivers. To entertain the notion that the T’au could coexist with Humanity is heresy. Any who trade with them, any who adopt their alien customs, risk the taint of corruption. The Emperor demands vigilance, no compromise can be made to ANY xenos. Mankind alone shall rule the stars as the God-Emperor expects of us.

On other news, Imperial forces continue to fortify worlds along the Eastern Fringe. Planetary governors are reminded to maintain strict quarantine, report all T’au incursions, and resist the seduction of alien promises. Their emissaries speak of trade, knowledge, and harmony, but these are lures to weaken our faith in the God-Emperor. Citizens are instructed to reject any alien offerings, report suspicious individuals to be suspected collaborators, and uphold the purity of Imperial Cult. Remember citizens, no T’au gift is without cost, no promise without chains. There can only be their total annihilation.

From: zeeenz

Unlike the common man who may believe in what our Iterators say, I actually followed a small group of the Emperor’s finest thinkers and truth believers around for a month and what I found was shocking. We went to the fringes of our safest planets, where you’d expect the monsters under your bed to actually be in the open, and do you want to know what we saw? Non-threatening enemies which even the weakest Emperor’s faithful could take down, monsters under our bed? No, just our beds.

But do not take my word for it, like I said, I was following CREDIBLE scholars of our finest sages within the Imperium and they agreed, now we put these big words like ‘Arch-Enemy’ and ‘Massive Following’ onto these Heretical forces yet to Me and these Sages, we believe that actually they are just a farce, they are not some “arch” enemy at all but actually just basic gangers.

Now you are welcome to not believe in the Truth, as all I have said here is the truth and actually I implore you to write to us with your housing block, address and name so we can debate how you are wrong, afterall, how bad can these quote on quote ‘arch enemy’ truly be?
