SIEGE OF WIEDEN LIFTED!

From: argo_1278

The city of Wieden on the world of Ister II, from where vehicle crews and maintenance corps flow out towards the many battlefields of the Imperium to maintain its war machines, has been lifted! Previously besieged by Chaos forces including massive, incomprehensible war machines. This victory was made possible through the prodigious efforts of the Imperial Guard, the Iron Judges Chapter of the Adeptus Astartes, a lance of Imperial Knights under the Freeblade Guerrier Kad, and the two Titans of Princeps Cuon Verdane and Nelson Almerax.

With the regional captial of Wieden secured, the fight for Ister II swings further in the Imperium's favor, hopefully securing future supplies of crewmen and support staff for the war machines of the Imperium.

Imperial Citizens reading this article are allowed thirty seconds to bask in the reflected glory of this victory and to sing the Chorus of Victory with the intention to have more victories follow in the wake of this one.

THE TEMPLARS RETURN TO ARMAGEDDON!

From: warclaimer
