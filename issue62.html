Marines Malevolent Show Incredible Restraint and Kindness by Only Destroying One Underhive District.

From: blackmako.

The residents of Hive Marnex were awoken this morning by the holy symphony of bolter fire, flamers, and the screams of the faithless. Frightened residents awoke to armored, grey figures with bolters gunning down the faithless heretics of District Drayko, which had been overrun by heretic forces. Residents from neighboring districts have shown jubilance at the revelation of Drayko’s Purge at the hands of the Marines Malevolent.

“If the Emperor’s Angels thought my district needed purging, who am I to question? I’m just thankful they stopped at the river,” one hiver claimed. “Frankly, that district was a cesspit of muties. Thank the Throne the Emperor’s Angels cleansed it in fire!” another hiver stated.

Some squalid cretins of the fake media and a few local preachers have criticized the actions of the Marines Malevolent. Rather than being grateful that they sent the women and children out on trucks for evacuation. Seditiously, they claim that the Emperor’s Angels gathered them up and used flamers—which mind you qualified imperial fact checkers have assured is not the case! The women and children of Hab Drayko were simply reassigned off-world and are already underway in the void as we speak.

“The cult was believed to occupy Sub-Level 88 in District Drayko,” Colonel Varn of the Cadian 124th said. “The Astartes decided on Sub-Levels 1 through 100. Frankly, I applaud the surgical precision of their purge.” Our military sources support the Angels’ decision to purge the blocks. Many have cited that Genestealers are an insidious threat which infects the whole population. Governor Halix assures that the families are being properly dealt with and has assured the population that the mountains of burnt debris are little more than garbage being disposed of from the nearby manufactorum. Fact checkers have confirmed this is true; anyone who claims that these are the bodies of dead women and children is lying to you.

“They could have leveled the entire Hive!” says Governor Halix, looking over a formation of enforcers conducting inspections of the purged district. “Stories like this remind us of the mercy and kindness that our Angels can show us when orbital bombardment would be more considerable! Some might claim that the Emperor’s Angels were monsters, but that is sedition and deception! They were kind enough to even transport the women and children to the wastes for off-world evacuation.” Governor Halix has steadfastly thanked the Marines Malevolent for limiting the destruction to one district, refraining from executing planetary leadership, and only killing five hundred thousand worthless heretics. The local Ecclesiarchy has authorized fifteen minutes of prayer at mid-day to be spent contemplating the endless kindness the Emperor’s Angels have shown to Hive Marnex.

Magos Tips #1

From: astronomicalhorrors

So you're an aspiring Magos? Trying to become the greatest? Look no further than this up and coming series of tips which shall be instrumental in achieving the status of Magos! I, Magos Leo, have extensive experience of being a Magos, and shall be personally writing these to aid those in need as I travel planet to planet.

The first tip you'll need is to stand out. Yep, it sounds boring and stupid, but it matters. You can't be anyone if you don't do something new or crazy. So, how do you "stand out" so to speak? It's difficult, but you need to create something new. Not just technology, mind you. Yes, technology is vital to the Mechanicus. But, to be a Magos, you need more than just technology, you need strategy. You need a method to the madness. So, I have to recommend that you make a new method. Granted, it might not be totally new. Someone else has done whatever you're thinking of, but that doesn't matter. The Imperium is giant, so whoever else has done it you probably don't know and never will. It could just be an evolution of something that exists, too. But, also, technology isn't just a new gun or tank. It's other things. Chemicals, sensors, biological machines. Granted, you have to follow the laws of Mars. Nevertheless, do something different! Not unique. Unique is impossible. But, if your Magos does his research by throwing bodies into a pit and seeing what happens... maybe do the opposite?

After the planet was cleared of the Ork menace, with the assistance of Kriegsman and Astartes forced, after-action investigations continued until the roots of this infection were able to be uprooted and put to an end. New training procedures were swiftly put in place to enforce better discipline and tactics.

Administratum forces continue to debate adjustments to Tithe grade to compensate for their cowardice.

ORDER RESTORED TO SHODROI, DEBAUCHERY DEFEATED

From: beardog2150

Thanks to the brilliant investigative work of the Adeptus Administratum, Adeptus Arbites, and even possibly the Holy Orders of the Emperor's Inquisition, the long-standing state of moral decay and disorder that ruled the court of the world of Shodroi has been put to an end! These horrid and lewd acts, detestable to all right-thinking and upstanding Imperial citizens, will no longer be a drain on the resources and attention of all involved.

This reporter was able to speak directly with Marshal Ijsbrant Platevoet of the Adeptus Arbites, to seek some insights into how these deplorable acts and their perpetrators were finally brought to Imperial justice. Apparently the investigation was a lengthy and involved one, hampered somewhat by the fact that while the acts are too numerous and scandalous to commit to paper, and are widely known across the entire sector, their evidence has been carefully hidden and specifics obscured from all but the most dedicated eyes. When I asked for a list of the ringleaders, or even the mastermind behind these disgusting and perverse acts, the Marshal sternly said that such knowledge was not to be made public, lest it tempt others into a similar fall - truly a thoughtful and appropriate response!

What is known is that this investigation, lasting 23 years and involving untold thousands, if not millions, of devoted Imperial adepts, has been brought to a close, with nary a whisper of the identities of the malefactors or their perverse deeds to spread their foulness. Planetary Governor Jenevra Cabra declared a day of gratitude to all of those involved, so that all may appropriately honor the hard work and dedication of these myriad Imperial servants in protecting and preserving the moral fiber and, indeed, very existence of the Imperium from this shadowy cabal. May the bells be rung a hundred times! Ignorance truly is our best defense!

Somewhere and Nowhere

From: mourhurst

On the frontier of an unnamed ice world, Imperial forces have waged a grinding winter campaign against entrenched Ork warbands, as elements of the 17th Bel Vor and Vostroyan Firstborn regiments carved a strong foothold across former city settlements. What began as reconnaissance-in-force quickly became a purge as many Imperial forces on the planet had a personal score against the Greenskins. Early clashes with roving Ork bands have been described as lightly armed and organized, which was surprising for Greenskins. Vostroyan engineers have improvised infrastructure from scavenged plasteel and prefab segments to sustain the force in the cold, while Bel Vor has executed the Orks in record numbers with losses barely in the double digits.

Thus far, no useful resources have been found for the Explorator fleet that helped bring these forces onboard. There is suspicion that this rogue planet has no actual use, which is draining the morale of the men who are starting to think the expedition is a waste of time. However, the masses do not know that most of the ground is so full of ice, that the prospectors haven’t even reached the dirt layer. There is still high hopes that this venture wasn’t for nothing and something of use can be extracted from the planet.
