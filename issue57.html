TROUT STOCKS COLLAPSE ON LAMIA PRIME

From: mourhurst

The damage has spread downstream, metaphorically and literally. With manufactoria silent or burning, Lamia Prime’s river networks have become an unintended casualty of the uprising. Over the past cycle, local agri-reeves and hydro-cartel monitors report a sharp and accelerating collapse in native trout populations across the Lamian basin, particularly along the Virex, Solenne, and Lower Bastion tributaries.

Under ordinary conditions, the planet’s trout stocks are modest, but locally are a prized protein for lower-hive markets, ration supplements for enforcer precincts, and a stabilizing resource for riverbound townships that have endured shortages before and survived by fishing. Usually the Lamian trout breeds faster than they can be fished, however, recent events have changed that and made a majority of the fish unsafe to eat and sterile.

Sabotage to filtration plants and pumping stations have allowed untreated runoff from Vantorus and Tymbra Hives to spill directly into the waterways. Chemical tailings, coolant effluent, and ash from manufactorum fires drift downstream. Many trout have been found floating belly-up against lock-gates and bridge pylons, their silver bodies dulled and blistered. In some stretches, the rivers no longer move with life at all.

The Magos-Biologis Ichthys who specializes in aquatic animals, confirmed that spawning beds have been fouled by heat discharge and particulate slag. Even where adult trout survive, eggs are failing in vast numbers. The social effects have been immediate. River markets in the southern plains have closed entirely. What fish remain are hoarded or traded at exorbitant prices, often under enforcer guard. Reports of skirmishes over netting rights and dock access are increasing, particularly as displaced hive-workers flee toward the waterways in search of food.

Governor-Marshal Voss’s administration has issued no formal statement regarding the rivers.

NO DATASLATES? NO CENSUS

From: The Editor

The decennial population census for the Lower Hives has ground to a halt following a "circular logic event" within the Department of Records and Redundancy.

The crisis began when the Office of Stationery Requisition requested three hundred fresh dataslates to record the census. However, the Sub-Committee for Resource Allocation denied the request, citing a lack of documented proof of need. When the Office of Stationery attempted to file the proof, they were informed that all "Proof of Need" declarations must be submitted on a Form 7-B, which can only be correctly generated by, and stored upon, a fresh data-slate.

As of this morning, three hundred junior scribes have been observed staring blankly at their empty ink-wells, trapped in a meditative trance of pure administration. The Department Head has issued a statement assuring the public that the census will be completed "eventually," once the population figures of all four billion citizens have been carved into the walls of the Sub-Level 4 corridor.

Citizens are reminded that being uncounted is technically a form of tax evasion, punishable by immediate conscription into a penal legion.
