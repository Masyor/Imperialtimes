UNEXPECTED DELAYS FOR ADEPTA RESPONSE

From: .rayja

Dear noble and pious viewers. An embargo placed on all trade and tithe shipments from the Agri-World of Synkidrax III will bring about slight delays in Administratum and Adeptus Terra response times in select sectors. This comes after a recent investigation concerning possible radical mutations in select serf families revealing a possible Xenos Infiltration, and thus the Arbites have decreed a total embargo on all non-sanctioned contact between the planet and the Imperium at large.

For those viewers who are ignorant of the purpose of Synkidrax III, it was an Agri-World noted for its production of Vellum and Vellum substitutes for the Administratum, and was considered a noted contributor to the administrative process in many more distant frontier sectors and regions in Segmentum Obscurus and Pacificus.

Rest assured dear viewers that while major delays are expected to occur in many more distant systems in the frontier, most systems will see only minor ones as the Administratum works to secure temperary Vellum sources until the purge is complete, and of course we at the Imperial Times are stockpiled on parchment to ensure an accurate and truthful covering of events in a timely manner.

The Emperor Protects.

"FAITH IMPROVES HEALTH" SAYS OFFICIO MEDICAE

From: wellbreadedfish

Eight solar cycles ago, Master Physician Hadrianus of the Officio Medicae announced that having strong faith can improve one's health. In a sanctioned Administratum study, citizens that were able to recall more verses of the Canticum Imperialis showed greater robustness and focus on completing tasks. Reeducation was given to citizens overseen by the holy Ministorum to improve their faith, in accordance with new Officio Medicae recommendations.

It is an unfortunate time of strife in the Imperium. However, the Administratum assures that with strong faith in the Emperor, Humanity can overcome any challenge. With these sactioned and approved results by the Officio Medicae, it is proven that the human will is our most powerful weapon, and our greatest tool.

To support the Imperial citizenry in these times, and in light of the coming celebration of Sanguinala, the Officio Medicae has begun to gift citizens embellished copies of the Canticum Imperials. These copies have been carried by Imperial Guardsman who served on the frontlines and blessed by Pontifices of the Ecclesiarchy. Each citizen can receive their copy at their nearest Medicae clinic along with a discounted checkup.

CAPTAIN ARORA DAVIS RETURNING TO DUTY

After suffering brutal injuries during the siege of Attrax II, one of the planet's most famed Captains was injured gruesomely in combat, losing an arm and a leg in the impact of an RPG. Despite the tragedy, her efforts had helped the survivors recover crucial data records from a command center on the frontlines, and helped turn the tide of the battle.

While Captain Davis was thought to have died in transit to more advanced medicae treatment off of the battlefield, duty rosters say she's returned to lead the men and women of the Astra Militarum once again. Not only has she returned from medicae treatments, rosters say that Davis has been promoted to the rank of Colonel and been trusted to command of the newly formed 21st Militarum Auxilia Infantry Regiment.
