010. Avoid Rhetorical Heresy

011. Regarding "The Farm"
If you find yourself suddenly relocated to an Agricultural World, do not panic. The nutrient-slurry is rich in minerals, and the lack of a vocal cord significantly reduces your daily carbon output.

100. Financial Devotion
If a gadget costs your entire family line, consider it a blessing. You are not "losing a lineage"; you are investing your descendants’ future biological potential into a masterpiece of logic.

101. Redaction
Should you see a colleague’s words replaced by [Sacred Brackets], do not ask "why." Simply accept that their original vocabulary was too inefficient and be grateful they have been optimised.

CRUCIBLE-WORLD OF CHAOTEI SAVED!

From: beardog2150

Praise be to the Emperor, and thanks to our abhuman allies! The Imperial smelting world of Chaotei, long a source of vital and unusual alloys, was very nearly forced to end its tireless and necessary work - but thanks to the intervention of a band of clever Squat guild engineers, it has been returned to full function!

Chaotei is a small world that passes dangerously close to its energetic star; no settlement can permanently survive its hellish surface! However, that same heat makes the world a natural furnace that would be nearly impossible to replicate elsewhere. In their wisdom and art, humans of ages past drilled great crucible pits into the world's surface, where common ores can be deposited - and after a day or two passing under their star, be remade into crucial materials for Imperial constructions! To load and unload these pits, enormous land trains cross the blasted and barren world's surface, racing to stay ahead of daybreak, their dedicated crews laboring to protect worlds across the sector!

Unfortunately, in these fallen times, the land trains have been slowly depleted by wear and accident, and with them, productivity of Chaotei has withered; projections were made that within no more than a few scant centuries, the world-crucible would need to be abandoned. Thankfully, though, due to recent diplomatic efforts, a Squat voidship of the Engineer's Guild passed within signaling range, and diverted to assist the beleaguered humans of Chaotei!

After a few months of work, the Squats have been able to service all the extant trains, and even promise delivery of additional engines! Of course, when they began to ask ridiculous questions like 'why not build underground' or 'couldn't voidcraft do this better', their inquiries were politely rebuffed. How naive of them to think they know better than the Men of days before how best to accomplish this task! Even so, we thank them for their assistance!
