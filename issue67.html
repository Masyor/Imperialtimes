PROPER AND PIOUS USE OF NUMERALS

From: .rayja

Attention, all imperial citizens, serfs, nobles, across all worlds and all inhabited systems inside and outside of humanities and the God-Emperor's Domain, be aware, effective immediately, that according to the recent passing of Lawful Edict CF4-523-D242, courtesy of The Holy Administratum, all Imperial subjects will now need to be wary and vigilant for unlawful and heretical use of High and Low Gothic numerals for unholy purposes up to and including:

-Disobedience to an Official of The Imperium
-Heretical Speech, Thought, or actions
-Summoning of REDACTED from the REDACTED
-Negligence of duty and service to the Holy God Emperor of Mankind
-For more see Administratum Edict CF4-523-D242

Unholy numerals that have been specially cited by the edict are as follows: VI, VII, IIX, IX, LXVII, LXIX, CDXX, II, XI, XVI. Others, if fulfilling the necessary clauses relevant to the edict, can and will be added in perpetetuia, meaning that any uses before the current and future edicts will be prosecuted as well, up to and including the offenders next 30-50 generations. To avoid improper and impious uses of unholy numerals in the face of necessity towards holy duty, follow each utterance with a holy prayer and advocation of the litany of Necessary usage in Ultimate forgiveness to the God-Emperor. If you or any associates happen to hear such unholy and unsanctioned utterances of unholy numerals, be sure to report the offender to your local Administratum scribe, Enforcer, Planetary Authority, and/or Ecclesiarchical Priest. Failure to ensure proper purity following hearing of such unholy utterances will be prosecuted with necessary measures such as Holy and Public Torture, Servitorization, and/or admittance to a penal legion.

Be wary and aware pious citizens, keep the God-Emperor in your thoughts constantly, and remember the bliss and wisdom of holy ignorance in the face of heresy and eternal damnation. The Emperor Protects.

Xenos Threat Intesifies In Italiano System, Ministorum Provides Relief

From: _coati_

Recent Inquisitorial investigations into the Italiano system has found undeniable proof of Xenos influence on Imperial worlds. The planetary governor of the system capital, Maxximus L. Ratio, has called forth Ecclesiarchal aid to assist in purging the heretical foreign influence.

Answering this call, the Ministorum has declared this threat grounds for a war of faith. Under recent declarations, pilgrim fleets will be arriving soon to provide relief to the system. After purging those who have succumbed to the Xenos' foul "brainrot", it has been stated they shall work on hunting the cause of this epidemic, and burning it at its roots.

Despite this, some nobles still remain skeptical of Ecclesiarchal intentions, stating they could be better used elsewhere while local enforcers deal with the "delicate threat". Diplomatic debates are ongoing, though religious action ensues.

TECHINDA GYRE CLEARED, SAFE TRAVEL THROUGH SANIA SECTOR ASSURED

From: beardog2150

May the bells ring a thousand times in thanksgiving! The infamous pirate system known as the Techinda Gyre has been cleared by the valiant voidsmen of the Imperial Navy, assisted ably by the judicious judges of the Adeptus Arbites! Thousands of renegades are now fated for execution or a penal world, and the Techinda system itself can resume its place as a vital merchant corridor rather than an object of fear!

Centuries ago, the Techinda Gyre was a curiosity, a system of tumbling rocks parading around a nearly-dead star, most noteworthy for its service as a waystation along a major trade route through Sania sector. Unfortunately, those who hide from the Emperor's light gradually made their own bases in the belts, and attacks on merchant shipping became more and more common until the system was ultimately declared a navigational hazard. Pilgrimage routes and shipping lanes were rerouted, leading to several worlds become backwaters and the overall time to cross the sector dramatically increasing. With that, came more pirate raids, and a steady decline in prosperity and security across the sector.

Fortunately, the Imperial Navy has heard the pleas of the common people! The 6th Battlegroup, joined by the 44th Battlegroup, 25th Hunter-Killer Flotilla, and 176th Judicial Task Force, staged a campaign of purification in Techinda! The fighter squadrons of the HDMS Magna Mater and Enterprize were vital in seeking out the numerous pirate bases, while the Arbites led the way in swiftly boarding and judging the pirate ships attempting to flee! With the Techinda Gyre reclaimed, now Sania sector can return to the peace and plenty of centuries ago! Indeed, it is said that the Navy may construct a depot in the Gyre to ensure that there is no repeat of this sordid event! A thousand cheers for the Imperial Navy!
