Memoir of Valhurard

From: absolute_bingus.

Sometimes nowhere is safe. The forge world of Valhurard was meant to be a point to rest and regain manpower, at least that was what the 124th Carlos McConnell, the regiment I hold command over. We were simply stopping by on our way to the front against the T'au forces when Orks made planetfall. Instead of receiving a trial by fire against the blue xenos, it was against the greenskins.

The invasion was swift, cutting off most of the planet, leaving the armies of the Imperium in small pockets across the planet. It meant that we would have to use careful tactics and brilliant strategy against the brutish orks. My forces were situated at an ammo dump with two other guard regiments, one being of more primative technology and one of standard technological level. However, when push came to shove throughout the conflict, our combined effort did push back the filthy xenos.

The orks first probing strike against us failed miserably. With the combined firepower of the guard, none of the xenos filth were left breathing. Afterward, we had the ability to break out and scour the nearby area, finding scared citizens of the Imperium and brining them under our protection. It was of small argument, however the brigadier general won out on rank alone.

It wasn't long though, until the green bastards tried tunneling under the depot to blow charges under the walls and flood in from there. Thanks to our superior tactics and the ferious skill of scions and felinids, the greenskins were repelled. Meanwhile, I led an attack on Orks that had been spotted setting camp on the horizon. The filthy xenos put up a fight, but the discipline and protection of the God-Emperor won out. While I once doubted the capability of the abhumans under my command, they have proven themselves capable of fighting feriociously in the name of the God-Emperor.

-- Vailen Herrick, Colonel of the 124th Carlos McConnel Regiment.
