From: muskovite

As the war on Cascadia continues drop troopers of the First Thalian Field Army came to rescue the the defenders of Ariadora Island. Which housed many powerful aquatic battleships of the Imperial Navy. In a grandiose combat drop, nearly fifteen thousand drop infantry of the 2nd Kavsky Air Assault Division landed.

Cascadian and Kavskyans soon greeted each other with open arms and humility. Quickly the commander of the defense of the island of Ariadora and Lord General Karanov meet and discuss plans to push the dangerous traitors and vile xenos off the island.
