HONORING HIS FAITHFUL

From: wellbreadedfish

To live is to serve the glory of the Imperium, and none can be said that there is no greater life than the one who has given it in sacrifice. We live in an era of untold heroes, martyrs, and saints who fight in the name of the Emperor. They bring His light to the farthest corners of the galaxy, purging the alien, the heretic, and the mutant. For there is no greater honor, no greater glory, than service.

But there are those whose faith is stronger than the darkness surrounding them, that even in death, it shines bright as a beacon for others to follow. On 8.201+NHS.M42, the entire 94th Nolgrian Shock Regiment perished while battling the forces of chaos in the Imperium Nihilus.

The 94th Nolgrian Shock Regiment faithfully defended their home world of Nolgria from an invading host of chaos spawn. Refusing to deny the enemy the satisfaction of fear, the 94th charged into the warpstorm with steel in hand, fire in their hearts, and fury in their eyes. Such was the ferocity of their hate, citizen survivors recall the colonel of the regiment, Vittoria Arrianus, holding the entrance of the storm by herself as she held onto the regiment's standard, refusing to let it fall. Further accounts tell that not a single daemon penetrated the 94th's offensive line.

Expedient action spurs faith and the 94th acted with honor and intent. Their explosive reaction to the chaos invasion allowed Nolgrian regiments to muster in time and stage a proper defense. The invading chaos spawn are being driven back slowly, but surely, in the Emperor's name. In honor of the 94th Nolgrain Shock Regiment's faith, the Bell of Lost Souls will ring in respect for their colonel. It shall ring out in reverence on 8.290+TSS.M42.

ORKS ON EXCRUCIA!

From: mourhurst

EXCRUCIA PRIME — The long-anticipated Greenskin incursion has finally descended upon the Excrucia Sector, yet Imperial High Command reports unshakable confidence in the world’s defenses. What some fringe agitators would call a “major invasion,” our commanders instead describe as a glorious opportunity to reaffirm the Emperor’s dominion over this sector.

Early this morning, citizens witnessed the brilliant spectacle of the Imperial Navy’s ships cutting down countless Ork vessels in the upper atmosphere. Defense officials have confirmed that thousands of Greenskin craft were annihilated before they could even make planetfall due to the valor of the Segmentum Tempestus Fleet and attached Astartes strike forces. Ground forces across the planet echo similar sentiments. Astra Militarum regiments, Planetary Defense Brigades, and local fortress garrisons report full readiness. The Death Korps is especially eager as the 85th Line Korps of Hive Death have drawn first blood against the ancient enemy.

Even more reassuring to the faithful, elements of the Ultramarines and Imperial Fists have been confirmed present on the frontlines. Reports from the western hives indicate that fortification efforts were completed a full week ahead of schedule, allowing Astartes leaders to optimize deployment with God-Emperor blessed precision. Citizens are encouraged to remain calm, stay indoors unless assigned to approved labor or defense duties, and continue offering prayers to the Master of Mankind. Morale remains extremely high across all sectors, and officials remind the public that panic is the Orks’ greatest ally.

RISE OF THE MILITARUM AUXILIA

From: _coati_

Abhumans have long been a foul stench on the greater Imperium, although with the fall of major Imperial strongholds, and some changes in the needs of the Imperium, this view is still shifting. Some Imperial Generals have gone as far as to praise their efforts and request their extended cooperation with localized regiments.

While clearly a matter of great controversy and debate, there have been recent efforts to increase their use as expendable troops. Recent tactics have involved letting the abhumans die as penitents in the most hazardous frontlines the Imperium has to offer, saving important guardsmen for greater fights.

In the last decade, over 6 abhuman regiments have been erected under direct oversight of up and coming Generals, alongside recognizable veteran Colonels, the newest of such being the 21st Auxilia Infantry Regiment, lead by Colonel Arora Davis. While some appreciate the extra bodies, others question the direction the Imperium is taking by allowing them more independent service.

For now, all the Imperium can do is watch with great scrutiny, and trust in the Guard to take proper action.
