THE PATH OF THE HOLY LEX: URASALIN MOBILE PRECINCT BEGINS ITS CIRCUIT

From: .rayja

Attention Citizens. In accordance with ensuring that our holy and truthful reporting never breaks accordance with Lex Septimus VII:1, we are pleased to release this story and to inform readers outside of the Blessed Segmentum Solar that The Hive Urasalin Precinct, based on the undoubtably loyal Proxeris IV, has formed and sent off a new Mobile Precinct and Circuit Court, headed by the honorable and pious Provost-Marshal Malchion Drex. For those readers unfamiliar with with the terms of the Lex, this mobile precinct plans to follow a pre-designated circuit route approved by one of the Blessed High Lords of Terra, The Grand Provost Marshal.

With this new force, the Circuit Court aims to enlighten and discipline those Lexless regions of the God-Emperor's Imperium, as well as providing righteous justice that may outpace the confines of a planetary precinct. Very soon, loyal viewers, may you see a visit from our noble Arbites, no doubt looking to enforce the holy words of the Emperor's Divine Lex, so do not fear!

We had a fortunate oppertunity to interview the Provost-Marshal before his departure, and he was graceful enough to provide a quote to enlighten you dear viewers:

"To all citizens of the Imperium, know this and mark it well. The Lex is always alive, always pulsating with the blood of martyrs and arbitrators who give their lives every second for our God-Emperor's holy justice. Like the Lex, we shall never sleep, never falter in our duties to the Pax Imperatoris. Be vigilant. Report any signs of Lex-breaking to your local enforcers, or face holy wrath for your insubordination."

HOLDFAST YE FAITHFUL

From: mourhurst

On the other side of misery there’s a world we all long to see, but the suffering we all go through now will be rewarded by Him in the end. To all you lost souls in the Vinta Expanse, once more the expedition says to you as we breakthrough the foe’s blockades.

Hold the line.

That is all the God-Emperor will ask of you all. By His grace we are saved, and by His hammer the foes of humanity will know the taste of defeat. You have not been abandoned, sons and daughters of humanity. Think of how your forefathers before you, have kept this Imperium standing for ten thousand years. That is no little feat. Take pride in your ancestor’s accomplishments. Think of how your parents, grandparents, and great grandparents, and so on are looking upon you from the Eternal Gates at this very moment. Seeing their noble sons and daughters being driven to the brink by the foe. Will you shirk from your duties to defend our Holy Imperium just because it is difficult? Or will you stand and fight against the abominable intelligence that pounds at your doorstep? By blood or sweat we will get to you. While the road seems dark and the way seems lost, the Emperor’s flames will guide ye faithful, your worlds are not alone. You will triumph. Reinforcements are coming. And if we are not there in time to bring salvation, then we will bring retribution for every martyr that fell in defense of His Holy Majesty’s Imperium.

The Emperor Protects.
Administratum Updates

TRUKK SALES REV UP IN ORKTOBER

Recent, successfully decrypted xenos vox-intercepts and captured logistical scrawls from the barbaric Orkoid enemy indicate a sharp, alarming surge in the self-described "sale" and production of ramshackle, crudely armored transports known as Trukkz. This spike coincides ominously with the end of the festive period they refer to as "Orktober."

Intelligence Adepts estimate a 25% increase in the deployment of these ramshackle "vehicles" across several active war zones, suggesting a massive, coordinated effort by various Ork clans. The intercepted data, written in crude pictograms and barely coherent scrawl, points to the Orks prioritizing "more dakka" and "redda paint" in their current manufacturing cycle.

The Departmento Munitorum has realesed a statement to remind everyone that there is nothing to fear from such crude machines. They are in fact, more likely to fall apart long before seeing any combat use.
