COMMODUS GROWS SILENT

From: dankest0130

In the wake of the passing of the terrible xeno menace, the so-called Great Devourer, which turned tail and fled back into the dark void from whence it came, cowering at the might of the Imperium’s defenders. Festivities celebrating the Imperium’s might spread across the sub-sector, with countless loyal and faithful citizens enjoy once in a life time privileges and spectacles in their already enviable lives. The Astra Militarum held many grand parades across the planets they were deployed to, many holy convents were established, and even a blessed few were taken as aspirants by the Emperor’s angels. Truly, the Emperor smiles upon this humble sector.

Since then, little has been heard from sub-sector Commodus, which now experiences a golden age of peace and prosperity. As requested by their governor, all trade, supply, and passenger vessels are to be redirected to other, less fortunate sub-sectors, such is their lack of want and the prosperity they experience. In other news, many battlegroups of the Imperial Navy have been mustering in the sub-sectors neighboring Commondus.

CONTACT LOST

From: amarain12

In recent months, Sovolanu was the site of a major deployment of Imperial forces, following a brief skirmish in orbit. The Imperial Navy quickly routed the heretic fleet, leading to a smashing victory, which was followed by the deployment of ground troops. This includes Astartes, multiple Scion companies, and a trio of Guard regiments, including the notable 501st Airborne Brigade, and the 70th Assault Pioneer Brigade. However, within hours of the landing, a massive heretic fleet returned, and forced the Imperial Navy to withdraw or face destruction. This left the deployed forces completely cut off, and their current status is unknown. The local sector command is anxious to reestablish contact with the force, given the value of several personnel on the ground, including a General. The Imperial Navy reports that a relief force is quickly being established to break the siege over the planet.

HOUSE BOUDREAUX OPENS TOURNAMENT FOR ASPIRING KNIGHT SCIONS

From: harrydugg

Forge World Amarth’s House Boudreaux has officially commenced its long-anticipated Aspirant Tournament, held within the mighty Forge Boudreaux. The event marks the beginning of a meritocratic trial to select three new Knight Scions worthy of donning the prestigious Helm Mechanicum.

The ceremony began with a thunderous blast of Warhorns, shaking the forge walls and signaling to all of Fuldera that the competition had begun. Banners of House Boudreaux and their allied Houses Presa, Faring, and Karel adorned the forge, reinforcing the solemnity and tradition of the event.

Competitors from across Fuldera and beyond gathered, ranging from local volunteers to seasoned representatives of different forge districts and planets. They assembled in the battle-scarred courtyard, ready to face the grueling tests ahead in ancient War Engines, including the imposing Aethon Heavy Sentinels.

House Boudreaux’s strict meritocratic tradition means any manial, Sentinel pilot, or Aspirant has a chance to prove their worth and rise through the ranks—potentially to the very seat of power under the Baroness herself. The tournament is not just a contest of skill but a testament to the House’s emblematic phoenix: rebirth through valor.

VIP seating is reserved for the High King of Fuldera, House leaders, and key dignitaries, while the crowds below eagerly await the battles and place bets on the promising future Scions.

The Forge World watches closely, as the outcome of this tournament will shape the next generation of Fuldera’s defenders.
Administratum Updates

The escalation of the Crossword War in Marki's Planet today has seen eight thousand seven hundred and sixty three out of two targets slain by Mechanicus-backed assistants of the local law enforcement. These temporary Enforcers have been quoted saying 'It is a good day to be a tool of the Omnissiah'.

The Adeptus Arbites has launched a widespread crackdown on unauthorized vox-channels transmitting "unapproved rhythmic chants." Officials state that only hymns to the God-Emperor and approved work-chants are permissible for public broadcast.

To support the ongoing war effort, the Administratum has levied a new "Duty to the Emperor" tax on all non-militant workers. The tax, payable in either Imperial Script or a proportionate ration of food supplies, will be used to fund new regiments.

In a day of solemn celebration, Manufactorum 32-Delta announced the successful production of its one-billionth cog, a truly flawless component now enshrined in the factory's primary shrine to the Omnissiah. The factory's Magos-Prime declared an extra ration of synth-ale for the day's workers.
