STABILITY ON THE FRONTIER

From: mourhurst

In the far reaches of the Southeastern Fringes of the Ultima Segmentum, there has been a record low in piracy, a feat credited to the unyielding vigilance of the Navis Imperialis and the decisive actions of several Rogue Trader Houses who have grown weary of the frontier’s lawlessness. These bold Lord-Captains, driven by piety, have joined forces with the Navis Imperialis to strike back against the criminals whose brazen and selfish greed sought to rob honest Imperial merchants of their livelihoods.

Rumors persist among voidfarers that the Emperor’s Angels have also taken part in the purges, silently descending upon pirate strongholds before vanishing into the warp once more. Though no official record confirms their involvement. Whatever the truth, it is clear that the xeno thieves and heretical brigands who once plagued these routes are no longer a threat. Local merchant guilds and trade cartels, particularly those operating along the Vornic Run and Helix Drift, have lauded the Navy’s renewed presence. Trade throughout the sector has increased by 28%, restoring the essential flow of luxury goods, valuable minerals, and agri-stock to frontier colonies. It is also mentioned that a few Rogue Trader families have invested a substantial amount in previously unknown expanses. Allowing the Navis Imperialis fleets to operate with a close supply line so far from the known territories of the Imperium of Man. Providing more security for Imperial colonists on the frontier from xeno incursions or raids.

Remember Imperial Citizens, the Litany of Disgust for the prayer of your planet’s cycle.

Each alien destroyed is a soul freed from eternal bondage. Each mortal alien life extinguished is a human soul raised to glory. Thus our eternal destiny is written in the blood of the alien. With sword and spear; destroy the alien. With cannon shot and gun blast, smash the alien. With laser and soaring plasma, scatter the alien to the stars. With tooth and fist and hammer blows. With axe and shell and poison and bombs. With virus charges and thermal mines. Kill them all.

CIVIL UNREST ON THE HORIZON?

From: The Editor

The persistent delay in announcing the winner of the Grimdark Document Contest has been pinpointed, not to heresy or sabotage, but to the Archival Adjudication Subcommittee for Standardized Seal and Stamp Compliance (AASS-SC). The committee has halted all final rulings, demanding that every single document submitted must first pass a re-certification of its submission seal's aesthetic conformity. Citizens, already agitated by the lack of sanctioned despair, are now further infuriated by the sheer administrative paralysis. The Adeptus Arbites maintains heightened vigilance, reminding the public that protesting bureaucratic precision is illegal.

MYSTERIOUS XENOS SAVE IMPERIAL DEFENDERS

FRONTLINE, GARRON VI. Just as heretic forces broke through Imperial defenses yesterday, strange figures cut through the clouds — sleek, flame-wreathed warriors unlike any known to the local Guard forces. Later identified as Aeldari warriors calling themselves the Cinderwing Corsairs.

They struck the heretics with blinding speed and vanished before dawn, leaving only scorched ground and vanquished heretics in their wake.

Imperial Command offers no comment, though whispers abound. Were they allies, opportunists, or something more? As one Guardswoman put it: “They saved us. But I don’t think it was for our sake.”
